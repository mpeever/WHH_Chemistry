\begin{itemize}
\item explain pressure as a physical measurement
\item explain the relationship between pressure and volume (Boyle's Law)
\item explain the relationship between volume and temperature (Charles's Law)
\item use the Ideal Gas Law
\end{itemize}

\section*{Gas Laws}
\marginpar{45 minutes}

\begin{itemize}
\item ``we can talk gas laws, or we can talk guns and rockets\ldots which one sounds better to you?"
\item introduce pressure $P = \frac{F}{V}$
\item introduce the Ideal Gas Law $P V = n R T$
\item show how it contains Boyle's Law and Charles's Law
\item mention partial pressures and Dalton's law
\item \ce{2KNO3 + S + 3C -> 3CO2 + 2N + K2S +\Delta Q}  
\item \href{https://www.usni.org/magazines/proceedings/1879/december/chemical-theory-combustion-gunpowder}{Chemical Theory of the Combustion of Gunpowder}
\item go over volume, pressure, temperature in combustion: draw on board
\end{itemize}
