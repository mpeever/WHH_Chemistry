\question[5] \ce{HCl} is an acid. Write down the chemical reaction that occurs when \ce{HCl} is dissolved in water.

\begin{solution}
\ce{HCl (aq) + H2O (l) -> H3O^{+} (aq) + Cl^{-}}
\end{solution}

\question[5] \ce{H2SO4} is an acid. Write down the chemical reaction that occurs when \ce{H2SO4} is dissolved in water.

\begin{solution}
\ce{H2SO4 (aq) + 2H2O (l) -> 2H3O^{+} (aq) + SO4^{2-}}
\end{solution}

%\vspace{0.2in}

\question[5] \ce{NaOH} is a base. Write down the chemical reaction that occurs when \ce{NaOH} is dissolved in water.

\begin{solution}
\ce{NaOH (aq) -> Na^{+} (aq) + OH^{-} (aq)} 
\end{solution}

\question[5] \ce{NH3} is a base. Write down the chemical reaction that happens when \ce{NH3} is dissolved in water.

\begin{solution}
\ce{NH3 (aq) + H2O (l) -> NH4^{+} (aq) + OH^{-} (aq)}
\end{solution}

\question[5] What chemical reaction that occurs when you mix \ce{HCl (aq)} and \ce{NaOH (aq)}?

\begin{solution}
Remember: \ce{acid + base -> salt + water}\ldots so: \\
\ce{HCl (aq) + NaOH (aq) -> NaCl (aq) + H2O (l)} 
\end{solution}

\question[5] What chemical reaction that occurs when you mix \ce{H2SO4 (aq)} and \ce{NaOH (aq)}?

\begin{solution}
Remember: \ce{acid + base -> salt + water}\ldots so: \\
\ce{H2SO4 (aq) + 2NaOH (aq) -> Na2SO4 (aq) + 2H2O (l)} 
\end{solution}
